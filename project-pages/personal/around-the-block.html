Around The Block

Overview

Around The Block is my most recent project that I've started working on, developed in Unreal Engine 5. I started working on this during the summer between my freshman and sophomore year at RIT. It's primary purpose for me was to be project that I would be able to work on in-between my time with other projects and school work. My focus with the (ongoing) development of this project is to make the technical side of the visuals look as appeasing as I could make them within reason. For this purpose I didn't model most of the environment, but the post-processing effects were made by me, utilizing Unreal Engine 5's post-processing volume system. The level, outside the main building, is designed by me. The gameplay is being designed by me, and I'm working on the story that the characters go through. Hopefully integrating it into other projects that I've worked on.
