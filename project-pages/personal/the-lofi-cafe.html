The Lofi Cafe

Overview

The Lofi Cafe is a project that started as a personal project, but I ended up using it as a project for an art class. It was one of the first projects that I worked on where I was able to focus on the art side of the project. I utilized several post-processing effects in Unreal Engine 5 to make the environment look the way I wanted. For example, the atmosphere in the world is very foggy, and the lighting is very dim, and the color grading changes when you step into the cafe. My contributions to the project was all of its functionality, including a custom inventory system and coffee making system, and some custom models, the coffee sign being one. The project was a great learning experience for me, as I was able to experiment with different implementations of making the environment interactive and visually appealing.
