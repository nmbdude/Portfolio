The Lofi Cafe

Summary

  • Started as a personal project and later became an art class submission
  • Prioritized visual storytelling with foggy ambiance, moody lighting, and localized color grading
  • Designed interactive gameplay systems, including inventory management and coffee-making mechanics
  • Created custom 3D models to enhance immersion, such as the neon coffee sign

Build Breakdown

  • Unreal Engine 5: Chosen for its advanced rendering capabilities and robust toolset, as well as my extensive experience with the engine
  • C++ & Blueprints: Used a mixture of C++ for performance-critical systems and Blueprints for rapid prototyping and visual scripting
  • Blender: Utilized for creating custom 3D models and assets to enhance the visual appeal and immersion of the game
