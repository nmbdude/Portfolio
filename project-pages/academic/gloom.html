Gloom

Summary

Gloom is a short, atmospheric exploration experience built in Unity. Focus on mood, lighting, and sound to guide the player through an eerie environment. Personal project centered on atmosphere, interaction, and level composition.

Build Breakdown

  • Engine: Unity
  • Language: C#
  • Focus: Lighting, audio, mood, and environment design
